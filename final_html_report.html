Introduction

Cyclistic is a bike-share company in Chicago. The objective of this analysis is to understand how casual riders and annual members use Cyclistic bikes differently and identify opportunities to increase annual memberships.

Business Task

The marketing team wants to convert more casual riders into annual members.

Data Source

The dataset used in this analysis was obtained as part of the Cyclistic Bike Share Case Study in the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate on Coursera. The trip data is provided by Divvy, Chicago’s bike-sharing system, and is made available for educational and analytical purposes.

Monthly trip data files from January 2025 to May 2026 were downloaded and combined for analysis. The dataset includes information such as ride start and end times, bike type, station information, ride locations, and rider category (member or casual).

Key Findings

  • Members generated significantly more rides than casual riders, indicating more frequent and regular usage.
  • Casual riders had longer average ride durations and were more active on weekends, suggesting recreational usage.
  • Both rider groups showed strong seasonal trends, with ride activity peaking during summer months.
  • Members displayed clear commuting patterns with ride peaks during morning and evening hours.
  • Electric bikes were the most preferred bike type, and popular casual rider stations were mainly located near tourist and recreational areas.

Recommendations

  • Target casual riders at popular recreational and tourist stations with membership promotions.
  • Launch seasonal marketing campaigns during spring and summer when casual rider activity is highest.
  • Highlight membership benefits such as cost savings, convenience, and access to electric bikes.

Conclusion:

The analysis showed that members primarily use Cyclistic bikes for regular transportation and commuting, while casual riders use them more for leisure and recreational purposes. By targeting active casual riders with location-based and seasonal marketing campaigns, Cyclistic can increase annual memberships and support future growth.
